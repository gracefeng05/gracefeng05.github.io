About Me

Before I was a coder, I was a musician — I've played the tuba and trombone since middle school!
When I'm not coding, I'm playing in the UCSB Wind Ensemble or composing digital music for my collaborative side project: Browo.
Naturally, I'm incredibly passionate about the intersection between computing and music/art. One day, I hope to code my own DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) from scratch!
